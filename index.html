Identity ✦ Carl Edward Sagan November 9, 1934 – December 20, 1996

  2. PhD in Physics from the University of Chicago
  3. Professor at Cornell University
  4. Hosted TV series "Cosmos"
  5. Advocate for space exploration and SETI
  6. Received the Pulitzer Prize for "The Dragons of Eden"
